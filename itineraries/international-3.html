Glamorous Dubai

04 Nights/05 Days stay at Dubai

  • Day 01

    Dubai Airport to Hotel transfer on SIC basis

    On arrival in Dubai, you will be greeted by your friendly Tour Manager or local representative outside the Arrival area at the airport. Transfer to hotel, check in and relax.

  • Day 02

    Desert Safari With BBQ Dinner

    Several activities joined together to form a perfect Desert Safari, which includes Belly Dancing, Tanura Dance, Camel Riding, Horse Riding, Quad Biking, Sand Boarding, and Dune Bashing. The tour includes a delectable Barbecue Dinner buffet near a bonfire, exclusive for the Evening Desert Safari. The tour includes dune bashing in a fully air-conditioned 4x4 vehicle.

  • Day 03

    Half Day Dubai City Tour and later on Dhow Cruise

    Viewing some of the city most beautiful landmarks. The magnificent Jumeirah Mosque built in 1979. Tourists must wear appropriate clothing - trousers for men, long sleeves and a scarf for women. Continue to the Jumeirah Public Beach, pass by the Jumeirah Beach Hotel and stop at the Burj Al Arab. Dhow Cruise: Along with the glittering moonlight Dubai creek aboard a traditionally decorated wooden dhow. While cruising down the Creek, take the opportunity to enjoy the view of the old and new Dubai with the tall glass and chrome buildings. A sumptuous buffet dinner will be served on board while cruising. Transfer back to Hotel.

  • Day 04

    Burj Khalifa with tickets and Dubai Fountain Show

    After breakfast in afternoon, you will be dropped at Dubai Mall, where you can have lunch on your own and visit the top of Burj Khalifa. Later Spent time at Dubai Mall and enjoy Fountain Show. Transfer to Hotel.

  • Day 05

    Departure

    After breakfast at the hotel, check out from the hotel. Transfer from Airport to the Hotel. Catch your flight back home with delicious memories of the trip.
