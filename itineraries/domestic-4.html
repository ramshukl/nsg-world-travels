Colorful Kerala

05 Nights/06 Days Kerala Trip

  • Day 01

    Kochi to Munnar

    Arrive at Kochi International Airport / Railway Station and later, transfer to Munnar. En route, witness the lovely waterfalls in Cheeyappara and the flowers in Blossom Garden. Check in at the hotel in Munnar and relax for a while. Explore the place on your own and later, return to the hotel for overnight stay and dinner.

  • Day 02

    Munnar

    After breakfast, proceed for a sightseeing trip of Munnar. Later, visit the sightseeing places in Munnar including, Eravikulam National Park, Mattupetty Dam, Shola forests, Kundala Lake, Muthirapuzha and Nallathanni. En route visit Echo Point, View Point and Tea Gardens. Enjoy your night stay at the hotel with dinner.

  • Day 03

    Munnar – Thekkady (92 Km / 3 hours)

    Relish a tasty breakfast and proceed to Thekkady. Check in at the hotel and relax for a while. Visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, Mullaperiyar Dam and enjoy boating on Periyar Lake. Facilities for night stay will be arranged at the hotel with dinner.

  • Day 04

    Thekkady – Alleppey Houseboat (138 Km / 3.5 hours)

    In the morning, after breakfast, proceed to Alleppey. Enjoy staying at the houseboat and take in the beauty of Alleppey. Facilities for lunch, evening tea, snacks, dinner and next day breakfast will be arranged here. Enjoy your overnight stay at the houseboat. Note: All meals included at the houseboat.

  • Day 05

    Alleppey – Kochi (53 Km / 1.5 hours)

    Relish a yummy breakfast, transfer to Kochi and check in at the hotel. Enjoy visiting the tourist attractions in Kochi and later, return to the hotel for dinner and overnight stay.

  • Day 06

    Departure

    Early in the morning, check out from the hotel and proceed to the airport. Enjoy your onward journey.
