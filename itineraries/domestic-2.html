Delightful Manali

03 Nights/04 Days Manali Trip

  • Day 01

    Chandigarh – Manali

    Meet & Greet on arrival at Chandigarh Railway Station / Airport & drive to Manali by road (300 Kms. / 8 Hrs.). Arrive and check into the hotel. Evening free to stroll on The Mall Road. Overnight in the hotel.

  • Day 02

    Manali Local Sightseeing

    After breakfast, proceed for full day local sightseeing of Manali like Hadimba Temple, Tibetan Monastery, Lord Buddha and Tankha paintings, Vashistha Temple for "Hot & Cold water Kund”, Club House. The evening is free for leisure and you can move around the Mall Road for shopping. Overnight stay and dinner at the hotel.

  • Day 03

    Manali (Up to Snow line )

    After Breakfast at hotel proceed for a full day excursion in Solang Valley, famous for its picturesque location and adventurous activities, enjoy lots of activities there like River Crossing/ Rock Climbing/ Rappelling / Paragliding/ Mountain Bike/ Zorbing/ Hot Air Ballooning / Pony Riding/ Cable Car etc. (expenses have to be at your own cost). Overnight stay and dinner in the hotel

  • Day 04

    Manali – Kullu – Manikaran - Chandigarh

    After breakfast check out at the hotel to drive to famous Kullu local sightseeing or enjoy white water rafting in River Beas (at your own cost). Travel to Chandigarh Airport/ Railway Station for your onward destination.
