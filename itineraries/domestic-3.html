Gratifying Uttarakhand

05 Nights/06 Days Uttarakhand Trip

  • Day 01

    Dehradun – Mussoorie(35 Kms,1 Hours)

    Pickup from Dehradun Railway Station/Airport, drive to Mussoorie En-route visit Dehradun Sheshtradhara, Late afternoon arrive in Mussoorie and check in at your hotel. Evening at own leisure. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Day 02

    Mussoorie local sightseeing

    After breakfast excursion to Dhanaulti which is 33 kms away to the ancient mountain top shrine of Surkhanda Devi, climbing upto 9700 feet / 2940 meters. Behold the Deodar and Pine Trees, beautiful valleys and snow capped mountains. Also visit the famous Kemtey fall, evening enjoy at mall road, back to the hotel. Overnight stay at Hotel in Mussoorie.

  • Day 03

    Mussoorie – Corbett (210 Kms-6 Hours)

    After breakfast check out from hotel then drive to Corbett National Park. Check-in to your hotel/resort. Evening free for sightseeing covering Garjia temple and Corbett fall. Dinner and overnight stay in Corbett.

  • Day 04

    Corbett – Nainital (65 Kms,2.5 Hours)

    After breakfast depart for Nainital by road. On arrival, check in to the hotel. Later, go on a sightseeing tour, beginning with boating on the famous Naini Lake (at your own cost) and visit the holy Naina Devi Temple. After that, visit famous tourist spots like Naina Peak, SnowView and Lands End etc. In the evening, enjoy shopping at the Mall Road. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Day 05

    Nainital local sightseeing

    After breakfast drive for lake tour of Bhimtal, Sattal, Naukuchiatal. Drive back to Hotel, Dinner and Overnight stay at Hotel.

  • Day 06

    Nainital – Dehradun (280 Kms,7 Hours)

    After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive to Dehradun in time to Board Flight / Train for onward journey.
